AssisMonter is a technology platform connecting users with professional, verified drivers who provide ride assistance with companionship services.
How is AssisMonter different from other ride services?
We focus on companionship and assistance, not just transportation. Our drivers are trained to provide friendly company and help with various tasks.
Are drivers employees of AssisMonter?
No, drivers are independent contractors who operate their own businesses and provide services through our platform.
